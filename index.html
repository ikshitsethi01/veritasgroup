Conferences and Projects

The Veritas Group has loads of conferences and events that is either hosting, or partned with. The following includes their information.

Veritas MUN

Veritas MUN is an online MUN that aims to reach people all across the world and engage them in engrossing discussions and debates through carefully crafted conferences including opening and closing ceremonies, an experienced executive board, as well as certificates of honour. At Veritas, we are dedicated to fostering a platform that values open discourse and critical thinking. We aspire to cultivate a community of passionate and informed global citizens who are committed to making a positive impact around the globe. Our organization encourages participants to question assumptions, seek pragmatic solutions, and approach global challenges with a discerning mind. We cater to the experience of all demographics– be it the beginners who require additional assistance or expert delegates who wish for further challenges.

Verizon MUN

We live in a society exclusively dependent on science for the fulfilment of all its diurnal necessities and skyscaping ambitions. Global affairs are vigorously affected by the developments fostered by science and technology, while science and technology rely on the resources and partnerships wrought by global diplomacy. It is therefore unthinkable to consider diplomacy and STEM as two separate, disconnected fields. Verizon MUN functions with this precept as its very spirit. The first edition of Verizon aims to create a convention that converges critical thinking and a scientific temper with intellectual empathy to stimulate change in a world that yearns for it. You can help us in organising an MUN conference that is everything but evanescent by registering now!



Kaizen For Change

In a world where vulnerable communities struggle to access basic needs, our organization stands as a beacon of hope. We provide aid, support, and resources to marginalized groups, addressing immediate needs like food, shelter, healthcare, and education. Empowering individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and inequality is our ultimate goal. Through sustainable solutions, self-sufficiency, and resilience, we strive to create a harmonious global community. With compassion, empowerment, inclusion, integrity, and collaboration as our guiding values, we envision a future where everyone thrives, contributes, and enjoys equal access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.
