Verizon MUN Socials

We powered, organised and managed the fabulous and extensive double Socials at Verizon MUN Maiden Edition. The Socials were spread across both the days of the conference and unleashed in a Four Phase Format - Opening Ceremony, Day 1 Socials, Day 2 Socials and Closing Ceremony. It included 4 Standalone Singers, 6 Bands and One dance group and is said to be the most enthusiastic Socials of Faridabad ever.

Heather Day Live

On 3rd December, 2024 the Founder of Veritas Group - Satvik Singhal along with Akshita Shukla performed for an extended audience of 600+ viewers live on the Instagram Handle of The Veritas Group. The event included musical performances, a fun QnA, and story-telling related to romantic encounters to embrace the theme of the day - Love.

Team Excursions

We at The Veritas Group believe that it is Very important to keep the team motivated and inclusive. Therefore we organise occasional Team Outings and Meetups to keep up with each other in person and to keep the team more enthusiastic towards their work. We usually go activities like Bowling, Mystery Rooms and Shopping trips. One thing that never changes is our ritual of getting Starbucks and getting "Veritas" written on all the cups.

Open Mic

To Commemorate Menstrual Hygiene Day, The Veritas Group organised an Open Mic in collaboration with Project Kamakhya at Believe Dance Studio. This Open mic helped in spreading the notion of the NGO and gave the attendees a leisure evening of fun, games and music. The monetory collection from the event was donated towards Kamakhya's cause of supporting Sustainable Mestrual Health.

ColdPlay Watchparty

Since many of us were not fortunate enough to get tickets to the Coldplay Concert in Ahemdabad for the Music of The Sphere's tour, we decided to do something about it. We collected Coldplay Fans from around India and organised an online meetup to share the music together as the concert went Live at Disney+Hotstar. IT was a surreal feeling for all of us as we vibed together to Yellow. Without a doubt, Fix you actually fixed us.

F1 Watchparty

The Final Race of the 2024 Formula 1 Season was something bittersweet for all of us since it was the last time we would've seen Leclerc and Sainz as team mates and Lewis would never drive the Silver Arrow again. To enjoy the last race of an absolutely priceless era we organised a watch part and invited all the F1 Fans to come and share their love for the sport.

Auto Expo Visit

As Delhi geared up for the Auto Expo 2025, we decided to take our team to check out BMW's drifting and Tata's new concept cars. The excursion allowed us to connect with more Car Enthusiasts from around NCR and gave us an opportunity to interview and explore some of the bright minds behind the engineering of these futuristic designs and concepts.

Book Fair Visit

We got Invited to the World Book Fair 2025 by BriBooks as a few of our Team Members indulged in getting their works published officially and debuted at the Fair. At this event we got to exchange conversations with the author of The HIdden Hindu, Akshat Gupta who enlightened us about his journey as an author as he gave us signed copies of his book.

Champions Trophy Watchparty

India is a country united by Cricket. As Team India geared up for their match against New Zealand in the Champions Trophy Final, we put together a watch party to witness the event where India reclaimed their victory over the Trophy after a long wait of many years.
