Veritas Model United Nations is the flagship Conference by The Veritas Group that aims to reach people all across the world and engage them in engrossing discussions and debates through a carefully crafted conference. We spend over a year working on each edition of Veritas MUN going through hundreds of quality checks over all elements which come together to form this iconic Conference. We cater to the experience of all demographics- be it beginners who require additional assistance or expert delegates who wish for further challenges. Veritas MUN ensures the participants the highest quality committees, Executive board, meals, socials, and an overall experience. This is the conference that backs up our claims of raising the standards within the Delhi NCR Circuit.

Joint Crisis Committee Allied

Agendum: World War II in the Pacific: Expansionism, Retaliation, and Total War, comprising two simultaneously functioning committees in the form of Allied and Axis powers.

Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile

Agendum: Addressing the post DRS crisis unfolding mid-season resulting in increasing incidents, car retirements, and on-track instability which threatens the future of Formula One.

International Arts Council

Agendum: Discussion upon the protection of creators and artists amid the rise of AI-generated deepfakes, plagiarism, and the unauthorized use of intellectual property.

United Nations Commission on the Status of Women

Agendum: Examining Forced Pregnancies Under Pro-Life Laws Versus Women’s Rights in a World Failing to Protect Existing Lives.

Agendum: Addressing the rise of “Forced Biological Harvesting” and protecting vulnerable populations from predatory organ trafficking networks.

United Nations Human Rights Council

Agendum: Safeguarding migrant rights amid escalating ICE detention abuses in the United States and parallels in global immigration enforcement.

United Nations Children's Fund

Agendum: Mitigating the “Cognitive Crisis”: addressing the impact of algorithmic micro-targeting and persuasive design on the neurodevelopment of minors.

United Nations General Assembly

Agendum: Addressing global instability driven by competing national interests and the resource control race to ensure long-term international security.

United Nations 2.0 Reformative Committee

Agendum: Reforming the United Nations Security Council under UN 2.0: legal, institutional, and procedural pathways toward equitable representation.

World Health Organization

Agendum: Addressing the concealment of crucial medical information and scientific evidence during high risk global emergencies.

World Trade Organization

Agendum: Protecting global trade markets from disruptions caused by political sanctions and back-and-forth trade wars.

Lok Sabha

Agendum: Mandating minimum education qualifications and stricter criminal disqualifications for elected representatives to curb illiteracy and corruption in Indian governance.
