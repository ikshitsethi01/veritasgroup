Welcome to Verizon Model United Nations!

We live in a society exclusively dependent on science for the fulfilment of all its diurnal necessities and skyscaping ambitions. Global affairs are vigorously affected by the developments fostered by science and technology, while science and technology rely on the resources and partnerships wrought by global diplomacy. It is therefore unthinkable to consider diplomacy and STEM as two separate, disconnected fields. Verizon MUN functions with this precept as its very spirit. The first edition of Verizon aims to create a convention that converges critical thinking and a scientific temper with intellectual empathy to stimulate change in a world that yearns for it. You can help us in organising an MUN conference that is everything but evanescent by registering now!

Committees and Agendas

Verizon Model United Nations has the following committees for participation of delegates:

UNEP

The United Nations Environmental Programme (UNEP) Committee facilitates global cooperation to address critical environmental issues. This session's focus is primarily on biomimicry and biotechnological innovations as sustainable solutions. Delegates will be debating and designing strategies to utilize nature-inspired designs and advanced biotechnologies, developing resilient, eco-friendly systems for a greener future.

UNCSTD

With the Agenda as evaluating the role of science, technology and innovation in achieving sustainable development goals 2030. UNCSTD operates under, and gives advice to, UNGA and ECOSOC on pertinent matters relating to science and technology. It holds discussion to foster understanding and cooperation in science and technology between nations, and employ the transformative power of technology for inclusivity and sustainable growth.

IPC

Media shape narratives, influence perceptions, and guide opinions. With this great power, comes the need for responsible reporting. Delegates will be tasked to report committee proceedings, analyse the debates, and present their comprehension of the same through any medium of their choosing- vibrant art, perceptive photographs, or the vivid tapestry of their words.



WHO

WHO is dedicated to health and safety. WHO plays a focal role in coordinating global responses to health crises, setting international health standards, and promoting international partnerships to tackle pressing health challenges. WHO sets a precept for serving the vulnerable.


FCC

This distinctive committee is set in a speculative world years down the lane dealing with hypothetical issues that we perceive may arise. A potent committee that discusses global affairs and conflicts in a tumultuous future, where the delegates will address crises that transcend traditional boundaries as they exercise paramount authority.

FiA is the governing body for many prestigious auto-racing events and is an advocate for road safety, and the sustainable development and growth of automotive technology and mobility. UNODC (Online)

UNODC expertly helps member states comprehensively approach threats of illicit drug production and trafficking, and other drug related crimes as they endeavour to strengthen international action against these grave issues. UNODC tirelessly strives to educate people on the dangers of drug abuse and its consequences.
