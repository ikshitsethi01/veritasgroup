What is The Veritas Group?

Veritas Group, founded in 2020 amidst the pandemic, boldly empowers the next generation through dynamic leadership workshops, thought-provoking conferences, and MUNs. Despite the uncertainties surrounding its inception, Veritas Group has organized impactful events, including leadership workshops and quality conferences that encouraged participants to explore critical societal issues. These events brought together diverse individuals, ranging from students to young professionals, who were eager to collaborate, learn, and make a difference. The group has achieved remarkable success, hosting the Maiden Editions of Veritas MUN and Verizon MUN catering to an audience across the Globe. They also have Vercian MUN and Vimarsha YP amongst their Conference portfolio, Kaizen for Change as their official NPO and Verses as their Cultural and Creativity Wing.

Veritas MUN Chapter 2.0

Veritas Model United Nations is the flagship Conference by The Veritas Group that aims to reach people all across the world and engage them in engrossing discussions and debates through a carefully crafted conference. We spend over a year working on each edition of Veritas MUN going through hundreds of quality checks over all elements which come together to form this iconic Conference. We cater to the experience of all demographics- be it beginners who require additional assistance or expert delegates who wish for further challenges. Veritas MUN ensures the participants the highest quality committees, Executive board, meals, socials, and an overall experience. This is the conference that backs up our claims of raising the standards within the Delhi NCR Circuit.

Verizon MUN Maiden Edition

We live in a society exclusively dependent on science to fulfil all its diurnal necessities and sky-scraping ambitions. Global affairs are vigorously affected by the developments fostered by science and technology, while science and technology rely on the resources and partnerships wrought by global diplomacy. It is therefore unthinkable to consider diplomacy and STEM as two separate, disconnected fields. Verizon Model United Nations functions with this precept as its very spirit. Verizon aims to create a convention that converges critical thinking and a scientific temper with intellectual empathy to stimulate change in a world that yearns for it. We have introduced this Conference as an experimental concept within the circuit to introduce the trend of Theme-Based Conferences.



Vercian MUN Maiden Edition

As our other conferences lead the grounds within the Offline MUN Circuit, there was a void we left behind – A quality Online MUN to cater across the world. This was when we decided to start the Vercian Model United Nations. Vercian is our way of providing a common platform to get together people from different countries around the world to work on creating a Conference so well presented that it attracts delegations throughout the globe. With engaging committees to keep everyone active at an online session and Executive Board members who come up with the most unique crises and Guest delegate ideas, Vercian is a true blend of creativity and diplomacy. Vercian never goes out of style since there’s always the next edition on the horizon!

Vimarsha YP Pehla Adhyay

As we build conferences over conferences which hold discussions upon matters that relate to International Politics and Issues that Concern the world at large, we overlook the quality of debate that takes place within the boundaries of our very own country. To put a solution to that we present Vimarsha Youth Parliament, a mock YP with a parliamentary form of debate to harness the arguments and issues relating to the Indian state. With simulations of the Lok Sabha, different Legislative assemblies, and organisations such as NTA and the Bharat Press, Vimarsha YP promises a bold and engaging stage for youth who possess enthusiasm for the Indian Political Grounds.

Kaizen For Change

In a world where vulnerable communities struggle to access basic needs, our organization stands as a beacon of hope. We provide aid, support, and resources to marginalized groups, addressing immediate needs like food, shelter, healthcare, and education. Empowering individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and inequality is our ultimate goal. Through sustainable solutions, self-sufficiency, and resilience, we strive to create a harmonious global community. Kaizen uplifts underserved communities through education and skill-building programs too. With compassion, empowerment, inclusion, integrity, and collaboration as our guiding values, we envision a future where everyone thrives, contributes, and enjoys equal access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.

Verses: The Cultural Wing

Ever wondered how we manage to pull off such great Socials for all our conferences? How do we do such engaging public events like Open Mics and Watch Parties? The answer to those questions is – Verses: The Cultural and Creativity Wing of the Veritas Group. If debating and diplomacy are not where your soul is, and you rather would want to share your taste in music, sports, dance or theatre with everyone else and build a community around it, Verses is just what you need in your life. At Verses, we indulge in jamming sessions live on Instagram, creating inclusive Fan Groups to stream Sports Finals or Concerts together and engaging Open Mics to provide a stage for those who deserve it!
