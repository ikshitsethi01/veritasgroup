What is The Veritas Group?

Veritas Group, founded in 2020 amidst the pandemic, boldly empowers the next generation through dynamic leadership workshops, thought-provoking conferences, and MUNs. Despite the uncertainties surrounding its inception, Veritas Group has organized impactful events, including leadership workshops and quality conferences that encouraged participants to explore critical societal issues. These events brought together diverse individuals, ranging from students to young professionals, who were eager to collaborate, learn, and make a difference. The group has achieved remarkable success, hosting Veritas MUN 2024 with over 150 participants from diverse regions, thanks to meticulous planning by a dedicated 40-member team. Their non-profit initiative "Kaizen" uplifts underserved communities through education and skill-building programs too.

Veritas MUN Chapter 2.0

Veritas MUN is an online MUN that aims to reach people all across the world and engage them in engrossing discussions and debates through carefully crafted conferences including opening and closing ceremonies, an experienced executive board, as well as certificates of honour. At Veritas, we are dedicated to fostering a platform that values open discourse and critical thinking. We aspire to cultivate a community of passionate and informed global citizens who are committed to making a positive impact around the globe. Our organization encourages participants to question assumptions, seek pragmatic solutions, and approach global challenges with a discerning mind. Verizon MUN Maiden Edition

We live in a society exclusively dependent on science for the fulfilment of all its diurnal necessities and skyscaping ambitions. Global affairs are vigorously affected by the developments fostered by science and technology, while science and technology rely on the resources and partnerships wrought by global diplomacy. It is therefore unthinkable to consider diplomacy and STEM as two separate, disconnected fields. Verizon MUN functions with this precept as its very spirit. The first edition of Verizon aims to create a convention that converges critical thinking and a scientific temper with intellectual empathy to stimulate change in a world that yearns for it.
